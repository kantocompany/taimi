Key observations

  • GitHub Copilot Pro ($10/mo) is the cheapest paid individual plan. Mistral Vibe ($14.99/mo) is the only EU-headquartered vendor — critical for data sovereignty.
  • Team seat costs range 10× from $19 (Copilot Business) to $200 (Augment Max).
  • Usage-based is apples to oranges: Copilot charges per request ($0.04), Anthropic/OpenAI/Mistral per token, Devin per ACU. No standard metering unit exists.
  • Mistral's Devstral 2 output tokens ($2.00/M) are ~12× cheaper than Claude Opus 4.6 output ($25/M).
  • Cline offers zero vendor lock-in (BYOK) and now has paid Teams ($20/seat) alongside the free OSS tier.
  • On-prem enterprise: Windsurf (FedRAMP), Mistral (EU data, fine-tune), and Cline (self-hosted by design).
